The beginning of the AI journey

Before jumping right into the crux and components of machine learning, I would like to take you through its history and origin. It is believed that it originated back in 1959, when a computer programmer wrote a program to play chess. He himself wasn’t a very good chess player yet in the beginning he was able to defeat the computer as it would mostly play the same type of moves. But with more and more matches the computer started registering the good and bad box position and after millions of matches it became so efficient that it was able to beat the programmer.
